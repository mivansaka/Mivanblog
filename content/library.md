A curated collection of books, essays, and resources that have shaped my thinking.

## Books

### Technology & Society
- *The Shallows* by Nicholas Carr — How the internet is changing our brains
- *Understanding Media* by Marshall McLuhan — The medium is the message
- *Computing Machinery and Intelligence* by Alan Turing — Can machines think?

### Design & Craft
- *The Elements of Typographic Style* by Robert Bringhurst — Essential for anyone who works with text
- *The Visual Display of Quantitative Information* by Edward Tufte — Data visualization before it was a field

### Philosophy
- *Meditations* by Marcus Aurelius — Stoic philosophy for turbulent times
- *The Analects* by Confucius — Ancient wisdom that remains relevant
- *Letters from a Stoic* by Seneca — Practical philosophy for daily life

## Essays & Articles

- *"As We May Think"* by Vannevar Bush (1945) — The vision that inspired hypertext
- *"A Mathematical Theory of Communication"* by Claude Shannon (1948) — The foundation of information theory
- *"Computing Machinery and Intelligence"* by Alan Turing (1950) — The Turing Test and beyond
